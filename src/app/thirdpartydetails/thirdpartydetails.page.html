Third Party

Third party motor insurance provides cover for the following:

1. Damage to third party property up to a limit of N1,000,000     

2. Unlimited (but reasonable) liability for death / bodily injury to third parties.

To buy product, you will need:

  • Chassis Number
  • Engine Number
  • Vehicle Registration Number

You can find these on your vehicle license document which you are required to upload on your account after purchase.
